Testing: "Photography Play" stack tile

5 variations · one feature · hover each tile · randomized photos every reload

The shared idea across all five: a small stack of photos that comes apart when your cursor enters, then leads into the Photography Play gallery. Each loads different random shots, so reload to see the "couple of photos at once" effect. What differs is how the stack comes apart — neat fan, hand-of-cards arc, loose toss, cursor-led, or full explode-into-gallery.
